Making the diet stick

You don't need perfection for 4 weeks — just consistency. These are the levers that matter most.

  • 1
    Protein first, at every mealAim for 25–30g per meal — the biggest lever for staying full on fewer calories.
  • 2
    Plan one meal you reusePick 2–3 meals you like and rotate them. Decision fatigue wrecks most diets, not willpower.
  • 3
    Log loosely, not perfectlyA rough daily estimate beats a perfect log for one week then nothing.
  • 4
    Don't fear carbs around trainingThey fuel performance and recovery on lifting days, inside your calorie target.
  • 5
    Think weekly, not dailyOne higher day doesn't need "fixing" with a crash day after — just return to plan at the next meal.

Weekly weight log

Weigh in once a week, same conditions each time (morning, post-bathroom, pre-food). Trend over weeks matters more than any single number.
